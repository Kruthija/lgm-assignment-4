"RECIPE OF SEMIYA PAYSASAM"



payasam is a sweet dish and type of wet pudding popular in the Indian subcontinent.

it can also be called as:

  • semiya
  • payesh
  • ksheeram

Ingredients required:

ingredient name quantity
ghee1 tablespoon
cashewnuts12 to 15
sweet raisins1 to 2 tablespoons
vermicelli3/4 cup
milk4 cups
sugar1/2 cup
cardamom powder1/2 teaspoon

Step-wise procedure:
  1. Heat a heavy bottom pan with 1 tablespoon ghee
  2. Add 12 to 15 cashew nuts and fry till lightly golden and take them in a plate
  3. Next add 1 to 2 tablespoons of sweet raisins.
  4. Saut till they are turn plump
  5. Take them out from the pan and place it on a plate
  6. Add another tablespoon of ghee(optional) and vermicelli to the same pan
  7. Roast the semiya till slightly golden on a medium flame.
  8. Pour 4 cups of milk to the pot
  9. Bring the milk to a boil on a low to medium flame.Stir and make sure there are no lumps.
  10. (optional)You can add presoaked sabundana/sago at this stage
  11. Keep stirring and cook on a medium heat to prevent burning
  12. Cook till the vermicelli is softly cooked to prevent burning
  13. Add 1/2 cup sugar and cook on a low heat until the semiya payasam turns thickk.This may take time about 3 to 5 mins.
  14. Next add 1/2 teaspoon cardamom ppwder. Turn off the heat when it is of a thick pouring consistency
  15. Semiya payasam will thicken upon cooling
  16. Garnish with the fried nuts and raisins
  17. Take the required amount in a bowl and enjoy eating semiya payasam
